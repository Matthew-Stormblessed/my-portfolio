# Transition Into AI

Matthew became interested in AI engineering because he believes AI is one of the most significant shifts currently happening in software development. He wanted to stay current with modern technologies while learning how AI can solve real-world problems.

Rather than only taking courses, Matthew focused on building complete AI-powered applications. Through personal projects, he has gained experience with embeddings, semantic search, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), AI agents, vector databases, and prompt engineering.

His goal is to combine his professional frontend engineering experience with modern AI technologies to build intelligent applications that are useful, reliable, and enjoyable to use.
