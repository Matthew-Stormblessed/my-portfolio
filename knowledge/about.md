# About Matthew Johnson

Matthew Johnson is a software engineer with three years of professional experience building modern web applications and a growing specialization in AI application development.

He enjoys building software from the ground up and is comfortable owning projects from architecture through deployment. His experience includes frontend development with React and Next.js, backend APIs with Node.js and Express, and AI-powered applications using OpenAI, embeddings, vector databases, and agent-based workflows.

Matthew enjoys solving difficult engineering problems and learning new technologies. His approach is to understand how systems work rather than simply using libraries, allowing him to quickly adapt to unfamiliar technologies and challenges.

Outside of work, Matthew enjoys building personal projects that explore practical uses of AI and modern web technologies.
